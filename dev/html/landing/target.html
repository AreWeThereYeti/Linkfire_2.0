Advanced targeting
for a global audience

Combined with deeplinking, this is a powerful tool for timed pre-releases, country specific releases or device specific campaigns.

We provide the options, you set up the combinations.

Location

Route fans to music based on their physical location. Great for e.g. iTunes with multiple country url's.

Time

Select time intervals to route your fans to specific pre-releases or timed campaigns.

Device

Route desktop users to one destination, and mobile users somewhere else. Combinations are endless.
